I remember when I first arrived in the United States.
Even before the plane landed, the little windows
in the airplane revealed snow and ice-covered houses
and buildings. As I walked off the plane, cold air
crept through the corrugated ramp that led to the
airport terminal. Some people inside the airport were
wearing big coats and hats, which I had seen on television,
but never up close. I felt a little dizzy and needed to sit
down, and then my cell phone rang. It was my Aunt Sophia.
